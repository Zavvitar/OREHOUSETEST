ABOUT
Opened in 2006, The Ore House is Selby's first purpose built music studio. Our goal is to provide the highest quality lessons in a creative environment. Lessons with us are not boring or old fashioned, you get to learn your favourite riffs, solos and beats, as well as the theory that takes your playing to the next level. Our method of teaching is not only great fun, it will give you the experience and confidence to play with other musicians, as you are taught to jam along with other instruments.
